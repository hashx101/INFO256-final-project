Theme finder usability study

Thanks for trying out the theme finder usability study. This study will probably take you around 5 minutes.

Your Background

Do you have a college-level background in English Language or Literature?

The Task

You will be given a description of a literary theme, and two examples of it. Your goal will be to find more relevant examples of the theme.

Time Limit

There will be a time limit of 5 minutes.

Expert Evaluation

Try to find the best examples you can, and mark them as relevant. After everyone has finished participating, the results will be judged by literary experts on whether they are good examples of the theme.

Self Evaluation

At the end, we'll ask your opinion on the experience you just had.
